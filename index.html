Instructions

General Instructions:

  1. The number, type and pattern of questions and as well as sequence of sections in the Mock Exam are only indicative and these are subject to variations from year to year as per the CAT authorities.
  2. The test has 3 (three) sections. The total duration of the test is 120 minutes. PwD candidates will have 13 minutes and 20 seconds extra time for each section.
  3. Each section has (3) three sections. The total duration of each section is 40 minutes and 20 seconds extra time. So, PwD candidates will have extra time of 53 minutes and 20 seconds for each section. The same process will be repeated for the other two sections. On submission of all three sections, a summary of all your answers will be displayed on your screen.
  4. You will be free to answer the questions within a section at any point of time before the clock runs out. The server clock at the top right corner of the screen will start with the commencement of the test for each section. On completion of a section the answer of the questions will be saved automatically.
  5. You will be allowed to leave the test hall only after a minimum of 120 minutes.
  6. The question paper will have a mix of Multiple Choice Question (MCQ) type with options and Non-MCQ type.
